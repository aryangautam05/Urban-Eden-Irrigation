Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by contacting us via phone or email, or by submitting a request through our website contact forms. We offer phone, video, and onsite consultations.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve New York City and surrounding areas but can provide remote consultation services across the United States.

Do you provide installation services?

We specialize in consulting and design. We partner with trusted local contractors for installation services. We can connect you with reliable installers if needed.

Are your irrigation solutions environmentally friendly?

Yes, all our designs focus on sustainability, water conservation, and ecological health. We prioritize efficient irrigation tailored to local climate and plant needs.
