Privacy Policy

Your trust is important to us. This policy explains how Urban Eden Irrigation collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Urban Eden Irrigation services, website, and communication channels. Your privacy and data protection are our top priorities. We are committed to complying with data protection laws and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

By using our services and website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly to us when using our services, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Feedback, inquiries, and communications submitted via contact forms
  • Usage data such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information collected automatically
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and site functionality

All data collected is used in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide helps us to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Improve the content, functionality, and performance of our website and services
  • Send you important updates, notices, and offerings relevant to your interest
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Remember user preferences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in website operation and service delivery under confidentiality agreements. We do not disclose your personal information to outside parties for marketing without your explicit consent.

Disclosure is made only when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include secure servers, encryption, firewalls, and regular security reviews.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request restrictions on data processing
  • Withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware of such data, we take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post changes on this page with updated revision dates. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
